Good to know

FAQ

Hotels

We have a few smaller celebrations here in New Cairo before the main day, so if you can, plan to be in town between 2–8 January. Our recommended hotel is the Helnan Landmark Hotel in New Cairo. A bit more budget‑friendly nearby: Royal Inn Residence. Want something fancier? Kempinski Palace Cairo is a five‑star option in the same area.

Getting there

Uber works well throughout Cairo and is the easiest way to reach the venue. If you'd rather have a private driver for the day, let us know — we can recommend a trusted car company.

Dress code

Garden formal, in soft romantic tones — think lavender, sage, dusty rose, and gold. We'll be saving the white for the bride!

Weather

Early January in Cairo is mild and sunny — expect highs around 19°C (66°F) by day, cooling to about 9°C (48°F) once the sun sets. A light jacket or wrap for the evening is a good idea.
